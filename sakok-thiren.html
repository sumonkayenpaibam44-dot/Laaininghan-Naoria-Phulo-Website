About the Book

Sakok Thiren is a spiritual and cultural text written by Naoria Phulo. It emphasizes the revival of ancient Meetei traditions, spirituality, and cultural identity. The book is regarded as a cornerstone of the Meetei revivalist philosophy, inspiring generations to preserve their indigenous heritage.

Key Themes

  • Revival of ancient Meetei traditions
  • Strengthening spirituality & cultural identity
  • Preservation of indigenous philosophy

Impact

The text has played a crucial role in shaping the religious and cultural revival movement of the Meetei people, making it a guide for reconnecting with their spiritual roots.
