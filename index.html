About

Laininghan Naoria Phulo (1888–1941) a Prophet and a spiritual leader, philosopher, and revivalist of the Meetei community. He worked tirelessly for cultural and religious revival, re-establishing ancient traditions, spirituality, and the identity of the Meetei people.
